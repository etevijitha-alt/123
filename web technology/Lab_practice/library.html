About Us

The State Central Library, Bangalore, located in Cubbon Park, is one of the oldest and most important public libraries in Karnataka, established in 1915. The iconic red building houses a large collection of books in literature, science, technology, history, and competitive exam materials. It provides a peaceful reading environment and offers membership facilities for borrowing books. The library plays a significant role in promoting reading habits and supporting students, researchers, and the general public.

Our mission is to encourage reading habits and support academic excellence.
